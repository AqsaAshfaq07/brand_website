OUR LIFE STORY

Our brand, "LUXE" was founded in 2005 by a group of friends with a passion for fashion and a desire to create high-quality, stylish clothing at affordable prices. Starting out as a small boutique in a local mall, we quickly gained a following for our unique designs and attention to detail. As our customer base grew, so did our business, and we expanded to multiple locations throughout the city. In 2010, we took the next step and launched our online store, allowing customers from all over the world to shop with us. This move proved to be a success and our online sales quickly surpassed those of our brick-and-mortar stores. Over the years, we've stayed true to our roots by continuing to offer unique, fashionable clothing at accessible prices. We've also expanded our product line to include a wide range of clothing and accessories for men, women, and children.

In 2020, we introduced sustainable and eco-friendly options to our collection to align with our mission of promoting sustainable fashion. Today, our brand is a household name and we're proud to have a loyal customer base that spans the globe. We're excited to continue growing and evolving, always striving to bring our customers the latest fashion trends and the highest-quality clothing. Thank you for choosing Fashion Avenue, we look forward to being a part of your fashion journey

OUR MOTIVE

Designs are all about celebrating individuality, encouraging confidence and embracing diversity. Fashion can be a powerful force for good, and using it makes a positive impact on the world. Our clothing will not only make you look good, but also make you feel good about the choices you make. When women feel empowered, they can accomplish anything. Join us on this mission to empower women, one outfit at a time.
